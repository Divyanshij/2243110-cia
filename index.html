THE WORLD HAS SOME BIG PROBLEMS

The climate crisis. Gender inequality. Human rights abuses. Extreme poverty.
Problems of this scale can be overwhelming. That’s why we need a plan to tackle
them and that’s exactly what we’ve got. The Global Goals are the World’s To Do List.

This week, half way into the Global Goals era, world leaders are gathering in New
York for the UN General Assembly. They will be reflecting on progress so far.

We must take this opportunity to remind our leaders of the urgent need to redouble
their efforts to get the World's To-Do List done.
